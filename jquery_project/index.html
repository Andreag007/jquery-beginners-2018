A Kennedy Space Center Experience

By Andrea Giraldo

Hi, my name is Andrea Giraldo and I learned how to use jQuery. I used images from Kennedy Space Center to demonstrate different types of rockets and space related items. If you click on the buttons, you can unlock the pictures. If you click the buttons after they have been unlocked, you can hide them again.
